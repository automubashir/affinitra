Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 18, 2026

Affinitra ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website affinitra.pages.dev and associated social media accounts. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you visit our website or interact with our content.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to provide our services:

  • Automatically Collected Information: When you visit our website, we may collect standard web log data such as your IP address, browser type, device type, referring URL, and pages visited. This data is collected through our hosting provider (Cloudflare) and is used for analytics purposes only.
  • Cookies: We may use essential cookies to ensure our website functions properly. Third-party services (such as Pinterest and Amazon) may also set cookies when you interact with embedded content or follow affiliate links.
  • Information You Provide: If you contact us via email or social media, we may collect the information you voluntarily provide, such as your name and email address.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate and maintain our website
  • Understand how visitors use our website (analytics)
  • Improve our content and product recommendations
  • Respond to your inquiries or requests

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

3. Affiliate Links & Third-Party Services

Our website and social media content contain affiliate links to third-party retailers, including but not limited to:

  • Amazon Associates: We are a participant in the Amazon Services LLC Associates Program, an affiliate advertising program designed to provide a means for us to earn fees by linking to Amazon.com and affiliated sites.
  • Other Affiliate Networks: We may participate in additional affiliate programs (ShareASale, Impact, CJ Affiliate, LTK, etc.).

When you click an affiliate link, you will be redirected to the retailer's website, which has its own privacy policy and data collection practices. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these third-party sites.

We earn a small commission at no additional cost to you when you make a purchase through our affiliate links.

4. Pinterest Integration

We use the Pinterest API to manage and publish content on our Pinterest account. When you interact with our Pinterest content:

  • Pinterest's own Privacy Policy governs how your data is handled on their platform
  • We do not collect or store any personal data from Pinterest users who view or interact with our pins
  • Our Pinterest API usage is limited to publishing and managing our own content

5. Data Storage & Security

Our website is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. We do not operate databases or store personal user data on our servers. Any data collected through analytics is handled by our hosting provider in accordance with their privacy policies.

We take reasonable precautions to protect any information we handle, but no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website and content are not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child under 13, please contact us so we can promptly delete it.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to:

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us using the information below.
